⭐ Flagship Project

Kabiro Ward Road Network Upgrade

A comprehensive plan to tarmac, grade, and maintain all major access roads within Kabiro Ward. This project directly impacts every resident's daily commute, business access, and emergency response times.

✅5km of new tarmac road across key arterial routes
✅Grading and drainage repair on 12km of feeder roads
✅Installation of speed bumps at school zones and market areas
Planning & Design80%
Community Consultation100%
Funding Mobilization45%
Implementation0%

Timeline: Construction begins Q3 2027 upon election

Budget: KES 45 Million (County Allocation + NG-CDF)
