Our Agenda

The Kabiro Manifesto

Home/Manifesto
Maxwell Changilwa — 2027

A People's Plan for Real Change

This manifesto was born from listening — to market traders, mothers, mjengo workers, youth, and elders. It represents the real needs of Kabiro Ward, and our commitment to deliver.

01
First Priority

Youth Empowerment

The youth of Kabiro are the engine of tomorrow. With the right tools, platforms, and opportunities, they will transform this ward.

✅Establish a Kabiro Youth Digital Hub with free Wi-Fi, computers, and coding classes
✅Launch a Youth SACCO with seed capital for entrepreneurship and business start-ups
✅Organize quarterly job fairs connecting youth to formal employment opportunities
✅Partner with TVETs and colleges for subsidized vocational training programs
✅Establish annual sports and talent programs to identify and develop local talent
02
Women Come First

Women Empowerment

Women are the backbone of Kabiro's economy and households. Their prosperity is the ward's prosperity.

✅Allocate dedicated and affordable market stalls to women traders across the ward
✅Facilitate access to government biashara loans and county women fund for all eligible women
✅Establish monthly women leadership forums for policy input and civic engagement
✅Champion maternal and reproductive healthcare access at sub-county level
✅Organize women's financial literacy and business training workshops
03
Infrastructure

Roads & Infrastructure

Every road in Kabiro will be improved. Movement drives commerce, safety, and dignity.

✅Lobby for tarmacking of key arterial roads connecting Kabiro to main highways
✅Install solar-powered street lights along all major roads and alleys
✅Construct proper drainage systems to prevent annual flooding
✅Create footpaths and pedestrian walkways for school children and pedestrians
✅Establish a road maintenance committee for ongoing upkeep and accountability
04
Wellbeing

Healthcare Access

✅Organize quarterly free medical camps for residents across all sub-locations
✅Advocate for upgraded dispensaries with modern equipment and adequate staffing
✅Launch mental health awareness campaigns targeting youth and families
✅Facilitate NHIF enrollment drives for uninsured residents and workers
✅Support mother and child wellness clinics with essential supplies and trained staff
05
Basic Needs

Water & Sanitation

✅Install community clean water access points in underserved sub-locations
✅Upgrade and maintain public toilets and sanitation blocks across the ward
✅Improve solid waste management with regular collection schedules and skip placements
✅Create green spaces and parks for community recreation and environmental health
